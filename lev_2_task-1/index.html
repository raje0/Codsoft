Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The Great Maratha Warrior King (1630-1680)

The Lion of Maharashtra

The Founder of Maratha Empire

Military Genius

Pioneer of guerrilla warfare tactics and naval forces

Administrative Reformer

Established efficient civil administration with fair tax system

Religious Tolerance

Promoted harmony between all religions in his kingdom

1630 - Birth of a Legend

Born at Shivneri Fort to Shahaji Bhonsle and Jijabai

1645 - First Conquest

Captured Torna Fort at age 15, beginning his military career

1674 - Coronation

Crowned as Chhatrapati (Paramount Sovereign) at Raigad Fort
